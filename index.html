About Us!

At WildwoodWatch, we're dedicated to spreading awareness about the critical issue of deforestation and its devastating impact on our climate and environment. Our mission is to educate the world about how deforestation contributes to a staggering 10% increase in greenhouse gas emissions. Moreover, this destruction of forests disrupts the lives of countless animal species, forcing them out of their natural habitats and pushing them towards extinction.

Through advocacy, education, and community engagement, WildwoodWatch strives to empower individuals and communities to make sustainable choices. We work tirelessly to protect our forests and the precious biodiversity they harbor, promoting a harmonious coexistence between humanity and nature.
